# Stage 4 Localisation & Preliminary Health-Claim Wording Review Notes

The following product names, categories, and marketing phrases have been flagged during the Stage 4 **preliminary health-claim wording review** as high-risk therapeutic or medicinal claims under Tanzanian regulatory definitions. 

These will undergo systematic revision in the Stage 5 updates:

## 🚨 Flagged Compliance Wording

1. **"Immunity Boost" (Product Name & Marketing Copy)**
   - *Risk*: Suggests active pharmacological actions that alter the body's immune system, which is a medicinal drug claim.
   - *Proposed Stage 5 Remedy*: Rename to a wellness-focused title such as **"Winter Harvest Infusion"** or **"Seasonal Vitality Ritual"**.

2. **"Calmness Tincture" (Description Claim: "formulated for tranquility, rest, and stress reduction")**
   - *Risk*: Promotes active stress treatment or physiological anxiety reduction.
   - *Proposed Stage 5 Remedy*: Shift claim to **"crafted to support moments of relaxation and emotional balance"**.

3. **"Digestive Blend" (Description Claim: "designed to support optimal gut performance")**
   - *Risk*: Implicit physiological treatment claim for gastrological systems.
   - *Proposed Stage 5 Remedy*: Modify to **"designed to comfort digestive cycles after meals"**.

4. **"Botanical Balm" (Category & Wording: "Soothing Topical Relief", "soothe muscular tension")**
   - *Risk*: Claims active pain relief or medical/dermatological healing of muscle strains.
   - *Proposed Stage 5 Remedy*: Modify to **"soothing herbal rub for physical comfort after exertion"**.

5. **"Forest Elixir" (Description Claim: "clear mental fog and ground the nervous system")**
   - *Risk*: Suggests neurological healing or cognitive enhancement properties.
   - *Proposed Stage 5 Remedy*: Rephrase to **"promotes natural clarity and a sense of grounding"**.

6. **"Root Vitality Salts" (Description Claim: "Therapeutic mineral bath salts to ground and restore vitality")**
   - *Risk*: The word **"Therapeutic"** is a direct medical classification.
   - *Proposed Stage 5 Remedy*: Change to **"Refreshing mineral bath salts for post-activity rituals"**.

7. **General Terms Flagged for Revision**:
   - **"Remedies"** (in hero copy: "A curated selection of plant-based remedies...") -> Replace with **"Botanicals"** or **"Ritual Essences"**.
   - **"Healing"** (in category and filter headers) -> Replace with **"Nourishing"** or **"Well-being"**.
